Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

SmarterQuit ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at SmarterQuit.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Program data: Your intake answers (what you're quitting, how much, how long, weekly spend, quit reason) are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Craving logs: Your craving entries (strength, trigger, timestamp) are stored locally in your browser. We do not collect or transmit this data.
  • Payment information: Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Email address: If you voluntarily provide your email address (for example, at the end of the program), we store it to send you program-related communications. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data (such as pages visited) through standard analytics tools to improve our service. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the SmarterQuit 21-day program
  • To send program-related emails if you have opted in
  • To process and fulfill your purchase via Stripe
  • To improve our service based on anonymized usage patterns
  • To respond to customer support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

The majority of your personal program data is stored locally on your own device using your browser's localStorage. We do not have access to this data. Your email address, if provided, is stored securely with our email service provider and is protected by industry-standard encryption.

4. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Ever. Full stop.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • MailerLite / ConvertKit — email delivery (only if you opt in). You can unsubscribe from any email.
  • Vercel — website hosting. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the website to function. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not serve ads on this website.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Clear your local program data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: hello@smarterquit.com
